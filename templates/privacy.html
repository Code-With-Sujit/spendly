Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the information you provide directly: your name, email address, and the expense records you log (amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions). We do not collect payment information or government-issued identification.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and personalising your experience. We do not use your financial data for advertising, and we do not sell or share it with third parties for their own purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it against unauthorised access, alteration, or deletion. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for hosting and analytics (e.g., server infrastructure providers). These providers are contractually obligated to handle your data only as necessary to deliver those services and may not use it for other purposes.

5. Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time through your account settings. If you delete your account, all associated data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through the app's support channel. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
